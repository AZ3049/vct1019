Arri Zimiga Jr

Arri Zimiga is the creator of AZ Productions. Born and raised in South Dakota. Studies Film Making and Video Production at Century College in White Bear Lake, Minnesota. Past work has been on interview sets, video sets, and working behind the camera. Always been passionate for art, film, and music his whole life. He’s taken an interest in art since taking a pottery class in 8th grade and watching many films when growing up. With his production company he hopes to achieve making other peoples’ art come to life and keep getting better at what he loves doing.
